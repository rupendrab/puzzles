Quick Help

  • Choose a piece from the Piece menu.
  • Choose a color from the Color menu.
  • Click an empty square to place the selected piece and color.
  • Click a piece to toggle it between black and white.
  • Drag a piece to another square to move it, replacing any piece there.
  • Right-click or long-press a piece and choose Delete to clear that square.
  • Use Undo to reverse the last board change.
  • Use Clear Board to remove all pieces.
  • Use Download SVG to save the current board.
  • Use Download PNG to save a raster image of the current board.
