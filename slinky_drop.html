Approximate Formulas Used

\[ T_c \approx \sqrt{\frac{2L}{g}} \]

Collapse time approximation, where \(L\) is the initial hanging slinky height and \(g\) is the animation gravity scale.

\[ y_{\text{top}}(t) = y_0 + D \left(\frac{t}{T_c}\right)^2, \qquad 0 \le t \le T_c \]

Top motion during collapse, where \(D = L - H_c\) and \(H_c\) is the compressed height.

\[ v_c = \frac{2D}{T_c} \]

Approximate handoff speed into the rigid falling phase.

\[ y(t) = y_c + v_c t + \frac{1}{2} g t^2 \]

Rigid-body fall after the slinky has collapsed.
