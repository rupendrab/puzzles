Small Circle Rolling Around / Inside Large Circle

About This Page

This animation shows a small circle rolling either outside or inside a larger circle. The red line marks the rotation of the small circle, so you can compare the visible turning with the expected number of rotations after one full trip.

  • Set the large-to-small radius ratio, then click Apply to redraw the circles.
  • Choose Outside Rolling or Inside Rolling to switch the type of motion.
  • Click Start / Restart to begin from the top position.
  • Use Pause / Resume to stop or continue an animation that is in progress.
  • The animation stops automatically after one full orbit so the final rotation count stays visible.
