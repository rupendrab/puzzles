🎯 Drawing Lines

  • Click and drag from one tree to another to draw a line
  • A dashed preview line follows your mouse while dragging
  • Release on a different tree to create the line

🌲 Highlighting Trees

  • Right-click on any tree to open context menu
  • Choose "Highlight" to make tree glow red
  • Choose "Normal" to remove highlighting

📐 Managing Lines

  • Click on a line to select it (turns red and thick)
  • Press Delete or Backspace to remove selected line
  • Click empty space to deselect

👁️ Visibility Control

  • Check "Hide non-highlighted trees" to show only red trees
  • Useful for focusing on specific parts of your solution
  • Uncheck to show all trees again

💾 Save & Restore

  • Save State: Stores current lines and highlights
  • Load State: Restores your last saved configuration
  • Clear All: Removes all lines and highlights
  • States persist even after page refresh!

💡 Tips

  • Trees glow green when you hover over them
  • Lines get thicker when you hover over them
  • You can select multiple trees by highlighting them individually
  • Use Save State to experiment without losing your progress
