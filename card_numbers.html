Card Numbers

Interactive walkthrough of the puzzle logic and the step-by-step elimination performed by the page.

By Rupendra Bandyopadhyay

The Puzzle

You asked for it: Alice, Bernard, and Cheryl, the logic family, are shown three cards, each with a positive whole number.

  • The sum of the three numbers is 12.
  • The numbers are in strictly increasing order: Alice < Bernard < Cheryl.
  • Each person sees only their own card.
  • Alice says: "I don't know the other two numbers."
  • Bernard says: "I knew you were going to say that. But I also don't know the other two numbers."
  • Cheryl says: "Now I know all three!"

This page lets you change the names and the total, then follows the same elimination logic to show exactly what survives at each step.
